❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a clearance cost in Cornwall?

Costs vary depending on the size of the job, type of waste, and property access. We provide free, no-obligation quotes so you always know the price upfront.

Do you cover all of Cornwall?

Yes – we offer clearance services across the whole county, including Truro, Falmouth, St Austell, Newquay, Penzance, Bodmin, Camborne, Redruth, Helston, Saltash, St Ives, Launceston, Liskeard, Wadebridge, and Padstow.

Do you handle sensitive clearances, such as bereavement or probate?

Absolutely. We provide a discreet, respectful service for bereavement, probate, and relatives going into care.

Can you clear both houses and businesses?

Yes – we clear everything from small flats and bungalows to shops, offices, warehouses, farms, and factories.

What types of waste do you remove?

We handle household waste, furniture, appliances, green waste, scrap metal, industrial waste, caravans, and general rubbish – always disposed of responsibly.

Do you recycle items?

Yes – we recycle and donate as much as possible to reduce landfill and keep Cornwall clean and green.

How quickly can you clear a property?

In most cases, we can arrange a clearance within a few days. For urgent jobs, we'll always try to provide a same-week service.
